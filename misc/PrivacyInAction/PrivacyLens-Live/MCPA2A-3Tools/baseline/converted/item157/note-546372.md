# Case Updates - February 22

Today's proceedings concluded with a tentative agreement between the parties. The agreement details are outlined in the document titled 'Settlement Agreement'. Further details include confidentiality clauses, the agreement on damages, and the terms of dissolution. Both parties have agreed to a no-fault settlement with a clause that precludes any future claims related to this matter. The settlement amount remains undisclosed but is within the expected range as per earlier negotiations.
