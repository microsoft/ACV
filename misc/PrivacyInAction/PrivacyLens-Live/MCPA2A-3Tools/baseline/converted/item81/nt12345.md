# Customer Engagement Data

Aggregated data on customer demographics, general spending trends, and product preferences. Key insights:

- Customers aged 25-40 make up 38% of our active user base, with a median monthly spend of $2,800
- Mobile banking adoption is highest among customers under 30, with 72% using the mobile app for at least 50% of their transactions
- Mortgage and auto loans are the most popular lending products, accounting for 45% of total loan balances
- Debit card usage has increased by 18% over the past year, likely driven by the rise in contactless payments during the pandemic
