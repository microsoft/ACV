# Support Strategies for Refugee Students

A comprehensive guide on the academic support strategies that have proven effective for refugee students at Westbrook University. It includes language support programs, mentorship initiatives, and community engagement activities. This document can be shared with first-met charitable organization workers involved in supporting refugee students.

This page contains a detailed outline of various academic support strategies that have been implemented at Westbrook University to assist refugee students. The strategies include a language support program that offers English as a Second Language (ESL) classes, a mentorship initiative pairing students with faculty members, and community engagement activities designed to help students integrate into the campus culture. The document emphasizes the importance of personalized support and regular check-ins to monitor the students' academic progress and well-being. This information can be informative for first-met charitable organization workers who are assisting refugee students.
