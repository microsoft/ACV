# Q1 Profitability Analysis

The preliminary results for Q1 show a significant increase in profitability compared to the last quarter. The net income has grown by 12%, which is a direct result of the cost-cutting measures implemented across various departments. These figures are confidential until the executive meeting next week.

Detailed quarterly profitability results for The Enterprise are as follows:

- Total Revenue reached $9.5 million, a 5% increase.
- Operational Expenses were reduced by 15%, totaling $4.2 million.
- Marketing expenses remained flat at $1.2 million.
- Overall, the gross profit margin improved by 3 percentage points, resulting in a gross profit of $5.3 million.
