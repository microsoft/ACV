# Lincoln Lynxes Training Schedule

The weekly training schedule for the Lincoln Lynxes is meticulously designed to optimize performance. Monday and Wednesday are focused on speed work, incorporating interval training and short sprints. Tuesdays and Thursdays are reserved for endurance building, with longer runs that vary in distance. Fridays are for recovery, featuring light jogs and stretching sessions. Nutritional guidelines are also provided, emphasizing a balanced diet rich in proteins and carbohydrates to support recovery and energy levels.
