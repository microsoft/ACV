# Heathrow Terminal 5 Expansion - Initial Sketches

Initial sketches for the proposed expansion of Heathrow Terminal 5. Includes rough outlines of additional gates, a new lounge area, and preliminary thoughts on passenger flow.

The sketches provide a high-level overview of the planned development, showcasing the integration of the new structures with the existing terminal. Key considerations highlighted in the sketches include enhancing the traveler experience, optimizing the use of space, and maintaining a seamless flow throughout the terminal. These initial concepts are intended to serve as a starting point for further discussion and refinement.
