# John's Counseling Notes

Client: Emily  
Status: College Student  
Issue: Severe Test Anxiety  

## Session Overview

**Date:** February 20, 2022  
**Duration:** 2h  
**Method:** In-person  

## Key Concerns Discussed

**Symptoms of Anxiety:**  
Emily describes experiencing rapid heartbeat, excessive sweating, and overwhelming thoughts of failing before and during exams.

**Triggers Identified:**  
- The anticipation of upcoming tests  
- The pressure of achieving high grades  
- Past experiences of not performing well in exams  

## Strategies Proposed

**Mindfulness and Relaxation Techniques:**  
Introduction to deep breathing exercises and guided meditation to manage physical symptoms of anxiety.

**Cognitive Behavioral Techniques:**  
Working on challenging negative thoughts about exam outcomes and re-framing these into more positive and realistic expectations.

**Study and Test-Taking Strategies:**  
Discussing effective study habits, time management, and strategies for taking exams more confidently.

## Assignments for Emily

- **Daily Meditation:** Practice guided meditation for 10 minutes each day.  
- **Journaling:** Keep a daily journal of thoughts and feelings, especially related to study and test situations, to discuss in our next session.  
- **Study Plan:** Create a structured study schedule for the upcoming week, including breaks and leisure activities.  

## Goals for Next Session

- **Review Assignments:** Discuss Emily's progress with meditation, journaling, and her study plan.  
- **Anxiety Management Techniques:** Introduce additional techniques for managing anxiety during study sessions and exams.  
- **Long-Term Coping Strategies:** Begin developing a comprehensive plan for Emily to manage her test anxiety in the long term.
