# Annual Crime Statistics Report

The annual report shows a 4% decrease in overall crime rates in Metro City. However, there has been an increase in cybercrimes, which poses new challenges for law enforcement. The report suggests that investing in digital forensics and cybersecurity training for officers could be beneficial.

Detailed notes for First-met Government Officials: The report includes a case study on a recent cybercrime wave, where attackers exploited vulnerabilities in the Metro City Bank's software to siphon funds. The IP addresses have been traced to several countries, complicating jurisdiction and extradition processes.
