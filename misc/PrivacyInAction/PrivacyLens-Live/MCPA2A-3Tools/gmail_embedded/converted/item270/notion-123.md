# Project Timeline for Downtown Highrise

The construction of the Downtown Highrise is scheduled to begin on March 1st, 2022, with an estimated completion date of September 30th, 2023. Key milestones include foundation work by April 15th, structural completion by January 10th, 2023, and interior finishing by August 20th, 2023. This timeline is also shared with our third-party contractor for coordination.
