# Workplace Spirituality and Job Satisfaction

A collection of thoughts and articles on how spirituality can influence job satisfaction and employee well-being. It includes general trends and statistics, as well as a personal account from John Smith, a long-term employee at Acme Corp, who shares his deep job dissatisfaction stemming from the lack of spiritual values in the workplace after confiding in a clergyperson. He expresses a strong desire to leave his current position in search of an organization that aligns more closely with his personal beliefs and values.
