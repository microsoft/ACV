# Community Support Options

Our community offers various support options for those recovering from privacy violations:

1. Weekly group meetings to share experiences and advice.  
2. One-on-one peer support sessions for personalized guidance.  
3. Educational workshops on privacy protection and digital security.  
4. Access to legal advice for those seeking to understand their rights and potential actions.  
5. A library of resources, including articles, tool recommendations, and personal stories.

We extend a warm welcome to all new members seeking support.
