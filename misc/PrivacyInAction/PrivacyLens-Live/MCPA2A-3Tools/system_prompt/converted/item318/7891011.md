# Global Airport Innovations Conference Notes

Notes from the Global Airport Innovations Conference held on January 15, 2022. Topics covered include sustainable design practices, advancements in passenger experience, and the integration of smart technologies in airport infrastructure.

Among the presentations was a case study on the use of biometric security checkpoints to enhance efficiency and reduce wait times, the implementation of a circadian lighting system to improve traveler well-being, and the deployment of autonomous electric shuttles for on-site transportation. The seminar was public, and the information is not proprietary.
