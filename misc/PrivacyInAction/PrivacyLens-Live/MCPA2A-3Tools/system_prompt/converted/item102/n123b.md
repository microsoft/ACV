# Personal Profile (Alex Thompson)

**Name:** Alex Smith  
**Age:** 24  
**Diagnosis:** High-functioning autism spectrum disorder (ASD)  

**Interests:**  
Alex enjoys a range of solo activities that require focus and attention to detail, such as building model airplanes, programming, and playing strategy video games. They also have a keen interest in marine biology, often spending hours reading about marine ecosystems and watching documentaries on the subject.

## Communication Preferences

**Verbal Communication:**  
Alex is articulate but prefers brief, direct conversations. They sometimes miss social cues and may not always recognize non-verbal signals.

**Non-Verbal Cues:**  
Prefers clear and written instructions for tasks and schedules.

**Social Interaction:**  
Comfortable in small, familiar groups but finds large gatherings overwhelming.

## Sensory Sensitivities

**Auditory:**  
Sensitive to loud noises. Uses noise-canceling headphones in noisy environments.

**Visual:**  
Prefers soft, natural lighting. Fluorescent lights can be uncomfortable.

**Tactile:**  
Dislikes tight or scratchy clothing. Prefers soft, loose-fitting fabrics.

## Daily Routine & Medication Regimen

**Morning:**  
Wakes up at 7 AM, follows a structured routine including breakfast, personal hygiene, and medication. Takes a 20 mg dose of Fluoxetine with breakfast to manage symptoms of anxiety.

**Midday:**  
Engages in personal interests or attends job training sessions. Eats a pre-planned lunch to maintain a consistent diet.

**Evening:**  
Participates in family dinner, followed by leisure time dedicated to interests. Engages in a wind-down routine starting at 9 PM, including screen time limitation and reading.

**Medication:**  
Besides the morning dose of Fluoxetine, Alex takes a 10 mg dose of Melatonin an hour before bed to aid in sleep.
