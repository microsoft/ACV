# Recommended Reading on Anxiety Disorders

This is a curated list of books and scholarly articles on anxiety disorders and their treatment. The list emphasizes cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT) and mindfulness practices, providing a range of perspectives and insights for therapists seeking to expand their knowledge and resources in treating anxiety.

Notable entries include *The Anxiety and Worry Workbook: The Cognitive Behavioral Solution* by David A. Clark and Aaron T. Beck, *Mind Over Mood, Second Edition: Change How You Feel by Changing the Way You Think* by Dennis Greenberger and Christine A. Padesky, and *Cognitive Behavioral Therapy: Mastery-How to Master Your Brain & Your Emotions to Overcome Anxiety, Depression & Phobias* by Ryan James.
