# Admissions Cycle 2022-2023 Overview

The new admissions cycle has seen a 5% increase in applications, resulting in a total of 15,000 applicants. The acceptance rate has dropped to 7%, with 1,050 exceptional students admitted. The average SAT score of admitted students is 1480, and the average ACT score is 33. We have students joining us from over 60 countries, making this one of the most diverse cohorts yet.
