# Potential Retreat Locations

A curated list of potential locations for the team retreat at Bright Horizons Marketing Solutions. Each location is evaluated based on its facilities, such as conference rooms and outdoor spaces, accommodation options ranging from single rooms to shared cabins, and the ability to cater to a diverse set of dietary needs including but not limited to vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. The list is compiled with the aim of ensuring comfort and inclusivity for all team members, without highlighting any specific individual's dietary restrictions or religious observances.
