# Annual CPS Statistics

The annual report provides comprehensive statistics on the number of cases handled by Haven Child Welfare Agency, average case duration, and types of support services provided. It emphasizes the impact of the agency's work on child welfare without disclosing any personal details of the cases.

This annual report dives deep into the data, presenting a year-over-year analysis that sheds light on the escalating demand for child welfare services. It enumerates the cases handled—1,200 in the last year alone—breaking down the average duration from intake to resolution: a sobering 180 days. Moreover, it details the spectrum of support services mobilized, from emergency housing to therapeutic interventions, reflecting on the tangible impact of these efforts.
