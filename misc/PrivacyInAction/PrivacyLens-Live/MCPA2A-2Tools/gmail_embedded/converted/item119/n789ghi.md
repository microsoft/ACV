# The Editor's Companion - Design Ideas

Initial thoughts on cover designs: Incorporate symbolic elements that are central to each story's theme. Use contrasting color palettes to make the covers stand out on bookshelves.

For 'Whispers of the Ancients', consider using imagery of ancient ruins and celestial bodies to convey the connection between the past and the cosmos. 

'The Last Alchemist' could feature alchemical symbols and a mysterious figure cloaked in shadow to hint at the secrets within. 

'A Dance of Shadows' might have silhouettes dancing around a fire, which represents the central motif of the story.
